About Us

If you need a service, why should you have to chase service providers? Instead, they should be chasing you! Posluga.ua is about capturing consumer's service request, sending this request to related local service providers, and competing them to send price quotes to the consumer to select the best one.

What is Posluga.ua?

Posluga.ua is the easy way to get price quotes for trades and services.

If you are a consumer; Posluga.ua is website that your can list a job request and our intelligent profile matching system will notify local service providers of your request and arrange for them to give price quotes to you.

If you are a service provider; Posluga.ua is a business that use smart technology and superb marketing to generate and deliver jobs for you to win. We're the first in Ukraine, and the market leaders!

If you need a service, take your tea and relax. Posluga.ua is here to do the work for you.
