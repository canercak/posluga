Frequently Asked Questions

We’ve tried our very best to make Posluga.ua easy to use. But sometimes things aren’t quite as clear as they need to be. If you don’t find the answer you need here, you can also contact us.

Consumer Questions

Requests
Can I negotiate the price that is provided to me in a quote? Of course you can. Everything is negotiable. But be respectful and understanding that everyone has a business to run and livelihood to make. Most vendors are quoting well thought prices that correctly match the quality and quantity of service you desire for your job.
I haven’t heard anything back from my request. What do I do? That's very rare but if that's the case you might try sending a follow-up message. If you still don’t hear anything you can do another request to see the multitude of other talent Posluga.ua has to offer!
Do I pay for the service you provide? No you don't. It's free and will be free.
Do you perform background checks for those providers listed on Posluga.ua? While we do our best to address any issues that are brought to our attention; we do not endorse, sanction, or verify those providers listed on Posluga.ua.
Quotes
How does booking on Posluga.ua work? Once you’ve submitted a form about the service you are looking for, related local service providers will send you a quotes in 24 hours. After that all you have to do is to select the best offer!
Do I have to complete the booking through Posluga.ua? We strongly recommend that you do. If you are contacted directly outside our system, we encourage you to have them go back to Posluga.ua to send you an official quote or you can get a quote from provider's profile. Bookings that are not done on Posluga.ua are not eligible for dispute resolution, secure payments, verified reviews, or booking protection.
What should I do if the service provider does not show up for my event? This is very uncommon but unforeseen issues can arise. This is one reason we encourage that all bookings happen on Posluga.ua. If you book the service through our site and the provider does not show up, we can ensure that we will find another provider to cover the service as soon as possible.
What happens if I need to cancel a service after I’ve booked someone? You can cancel the booking before the event date. But if you want to cancel the booking after the event date then the provider still has to pay us commission from the total price as if the service has been given. So in order to not make the provider mistreated; you must talk with the provider to decide on another date, another service or any other form of dispute resolution.

Provider Questions

General
Does this site really work? Well, of course it does! By creating a profile, you are instantly closer to getting high-paying jobs and tremendous opportunities. We are constantly refining Posluga.ua to ensure we’re presenting the cleanest and easiest website for search engines to crawl and index. Not only do we benefit from search engine traffic, but we also do a lot of online and print advertising, trade shows/conferences, sponsorships of events, and have exceptional ongoing relationships with corporate companies.
I’m not receiving emails from Posluga.ua. What’s the deal? You’re probably missing out on our emails because your email service has blocked our emails as spam. Try adding noreply@posluga.ua to your contact list of your email provider/software.
Leads
I just received a request from a consumer. How do I know if it’s legit? The vast majority of requests on Posluga.ua are from honest people because we use mobile phone verification. However, as it is with most websites, bad apples can attempt from time to time. Use extra caution when dealing with requests which contain any of the following. A simple test is to call the phone number. If it’s a generic answering system, a disconnected number, or a wrong number be cautious. Please contact us if you are unsure about the validity of a request.
How do I respond to a service lead? Visit your Inbox and click on the request you want to respond to. Then you’ll be given three options: you can go ahead and give a quote, you can decline the request if you are unavaible or not interested, or you can send a message to request more information.
Does Posluga.ua charge for receiving or responding leads? We sure don’t. it is absolutely free to receive and respond to leads. We only charge %10 commisssion from the total amount if you win the lead. If you add your profile link to your website we will be charging %9 commisssion.
Booking
How does booking on Posluga.ua work? It’s pretty simple actually. When you receive a lead you’re interested in, just respond by sending a quote back. We’ll send that over to the client and if they like what they see, they’ll accept your quote. Then it’s booked! All that’s left is for you to show up and do your thing.
Are there commissions or finder’s fees to pay when I am booked through Posluga.ua? There is a service fee of 10% that is charged when a service is booked. This fee covers our costs associated with development and maintenance of the system.
Can I update or cancel my quote after I’ve sent it? Quotes can be updated or cancelled until the lead closes for bidding or until client accepts your quote. If the client has selected your quote and the service is booked then you cannot update or cancel.
What happens if I do not honor a confirmed booking? If you cancel a booking prior to the event date, you will be responsible for covering the Posluga.ua service fee. If you fail to appear at the event for which you have been booked, you will be responsible for covering the Posluga.ua service fee and your membership may be terminated.
Reviews
How can I change a mistake that a client made in a review on my profile? Any changes that need to be made to reviews must be done by the person who posted the review. Just ask the reviewer to log in and edit their review.
Can I post reviews myself if I have received them from my clients in the past? Allowing you to post a review yourself would render the rating illegitimate, so we do not allow this. Please invite these clients to posluga.ua to get quotes from you so they can write about their past experiences.
Do you verify reviews? We verify reviews whether they are not obscene, abusive, or fraudulent. I that's the case we don't show them up on your profile.
Can I dispute a review if I am unhappy with it? You may dispute reviews only if they are obscene, abusive, or fraudulent - even so after our verification.
